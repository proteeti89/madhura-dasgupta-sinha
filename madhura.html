About Madhura

A movement
born from
one bold dream.

Madhura DasGupta Sinha is the Founder and CEO of Aspire For Her — India's largest gender diversity movement, with a community of over 760,000 women finding jobs, launching businesses, and breaking barriers.

Madhura has spent over a decade at the intersection of gender equity, entrepreneurship, and corporate leadership. She has moderated conversations with global leaders, been invited to speak at G20, and is pursuing the prestigious Stanford Seed Transformation Program (Class 2025).

From Salesforce to lululemon, from BCG to the US Consulate — her partnerships span industries and borders, all unified by one mission: making India's workforce more equitable, one person at a time.

Speaking topics

What Madhura
speaks on.

01
Gender Diversity & DEI in the Workplace
Practical strategies for building gender-responsive organisations, inclusive hiring, and diversity-led culture change in corporate India.
02
Women Entrepreneurship & the Path to Scale
From first idea to global markets — navigating funding, ecosystems, and leadership as a woman founder in emerging economies.
03
Returnship & the Second Career
Reimagining the professional comeback. Why companies that invest in women returnees unlock an untapped talent pool and gain competitive advantage.
04
Women on Boards & Leadership Pipelines
The business case for gender-balanced boardrooms and the systemic interventions needed to build leadership pipelines that reflect the workforce.
05
Building Community at Scale
Lessons from creating a 760,000-strong movement from scratch — community design, engagement, and sustaining impact over years.
06
The Future of Work for Women in India
Macro trends, policy gaps, and what businesses, governments, and civil society must do together to accelerate women's economic participation.
